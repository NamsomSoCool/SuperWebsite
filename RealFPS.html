A 3-day jam FPS where you play as the UE4 mannequin fighting UE5 mannequins. Clear a level, then choose a graphics downgrade that trades visuals for performance. It is an anti-roguelite: the game gets worse on purpose, and you pick the tradeoff.

Anti-Roguelite Loop

Progression by sacrifice: trade visuals for speed and decide which downgrade you can live with.

Step 01

Clear the arena

Drop UE5 mannequins as the UE4 mannequin in short, readable rooms.

Step 02

Choose the downgrade

Pick a graphics setting to reduce. The game gets worse on purpose.

Real FPS is a fast prototype built around a simple loop: drop UE5 mannequins as the UE4 mannequin, clear a level, then choose a graphics setting to reduce. Each downgrade trades visual fidelity for speed, so progression is a constant tradeoff between looks and performance.

Gameplay Stack

UE4 mannequin vs UE5 mannequins in compact, readable arenas.
Post-level downgrade choice that trades visuals for performance.
Anti-roguelite progression where the game degrades by design.
Inverted aiming/shooting as a playful jam constraint.
Controls
R = Reload
G = Drop weapon
1 / 2 = Swap weapon
Ctrl = Crouch
